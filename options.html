Socialnamer

X, Bluesky, Pixiv, and Mastodon work out of the box. Add other galleries here to enable smart filenames on them too.

    How added sites work

    When you add a site, your browser asks permission to access it - approve it to continue. On sites that expose a standard per-post metadata endpoint, filenames are built from the descriptive tag categories; on others the filename is cleaned from the image URL. Removing a site revokes that access again.
